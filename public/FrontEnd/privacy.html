Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 27, 2025

Welcome to TaskTrek. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and username provided during sign-up.
  • User-Generated Content: Tasks, shop items, groups, and activities you create.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the app, such as login times (this is placeholder text, confirm you actually collect this).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the TaskTrek service.
  • Manage your account and group memberships.
  • Calculate points, populate leaderboards, and track task completion.
  • Send notifications related to your account and tasks (e.g., password resets, deadline reminders).

User Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data at any time.

How to Request Deletion:

To delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

jrvvito@mymail.mapua.edu.ph

Please include "User Data Deletion Request" in the subject line and send the email from the address associated with your TaskTrek account.

What Happens Next:

Upon verifying your request, we will permanently delete your user account and all associated personal data from our systems within 30 days. This includes:

  • Your user profile (name, email, username, photo).
  • All tasks, shop items, and activities created by you or assigned to you.
  • Your membership and admin status in all groups.

Please note that data deletion is irreversible. Anonymized or aggregated data that does not identify you may be retained.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at jrvvito@mymail.mapua.edu.ph.
