About TaskTrek

TaskTrek is a gamified platform for teams to plan, claim, and complete work like heroes on a quest. Earn points, unlock rewards, and celebrate progress with your group. Built for engagement, clarity, and momentum—whether you’re a startup squad or a global guild.

FAQs

How does TaskTrek gamify work?
Points, badges, and leaderboards reward progress. Tasks have difficulties (Bronze/Silver/Gold) with corresponding rewards.
Is it free to get started?
Yes. You can create groups, add members, and start tracking tasks immediately.
Can I invite teammates?
Absolutely. Share an invite code from the Groups page so teammates can join.
